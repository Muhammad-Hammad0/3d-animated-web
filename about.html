About Passionate Design Agency

Our mission

Our mission is to make market-leading design and websites available to all businesses.

With rapidly changing design trends and customer expectations, it has become increasingly more difficult for brands to remain up-to-date, not to mention being innovative, with their visual identity. With so many branches and complexities to design and web development, building and maintaining a high-end visual identity has become virtually impossible without a highly skilled team on the payroll, which may not make economic sense. That ’s what we ’re here for, to help make market-leading design and websites accessible to all without the challenges of recruiting a high-performance in-house design and web development team. We strive to be the one-stop design agency with the ability to deliver outstanding results across all areas of design within a budget.

We are committed to hiring only the top 0.5% (1 in 200) of talent and incorporating the most innovative tools and ideas into the work that we produce for our clients. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, speed of delivery and the genuine advice we provide throughout the process. We try to be as flexible as possible within your budget and deadlines. And last but not least, we care!

Why we're your one-stop-shop for good design

Strategic approach

We take a strategic approach to each of our client projects and pay meticulous attention to all aspects of your graphic and web design. Whether you are providing B2B or B2C services or selling physical or digital products, we will focus on what’s most important for you and your brand to create outstanding designs. Our graphic and web design subscription will help you develop and refine your design strategy, so that it meets the needs of your target audience and helps drive more conversions.

Proven value for money

We believe in overdelivering and providing excellent value to our clients. Our rates are derived based on thorough research of dozens of comparable agencies that deliver similar quality service and output. And our rates are set to be the most competitive among them!

Attention to detail

We pay meticulous attention to the tiniest details. Everything from the composition and colour combinations to user experience and page layout is carefully chosen in line with your brand guidelines and goals to achieve excellent visual balance and impact.

Experienced team

Our team consists of highly experienced branding, graphic, motion, UI/UX and web designers, project managers and web developers, each with at least 5 years of experience in their area of expertise. Every deliverable is checked by the art director with over 15 years of experience, the project manager and your account manager before it is submitted for your review. We have worked with a great variety of industries on both B2B and B2C products and services, and we are eager to pour all our knowledge and expertise into your project to deliver outstanding graphics for your business.

Guidance throughout

Our graphic and web design subscription is designed to cover all aspects of design, and many parts may have interdependencies, but we guide our clients through the process from day one, setting accurate expectations and aiming to under-promise but overdeliver every time! Regardless of the project, you will work with an account manager and a project manager, who will update you every few days, ensuring you’re always aware of the progress.

Flexibility and speed

We appreciate that most of the time, design needs are intertwined with marketing and sales activities, and a variety of assets may need to be produced on a tight schedule to avoid delays in marketing and sales. So we’ve designed our graphic and web design subscription packages to deliver the best outcomes in the shortest timeframes. We also pride ourselves on having a very fast turnaround speed by all design agency standards. Typically you can expect to receive an update from us every 1-4 working days (depending on the size of the request), so you’ll never be left waiting for long!

Handover and training

We aim to create long-term relationships with all of our clients so they’ll have a reliable partner to turn to whenever they need to make any updates down the line. Having said that, we believe in empowering our clients to use the new assets developed as part of the subscription on a daily basis so we provide all the access together with guidelines, detailed instructions and recordings to support this. So we enable our clients not to be dependent on us for minor updates, and draw on our expertise for areas that need the most assistance.
